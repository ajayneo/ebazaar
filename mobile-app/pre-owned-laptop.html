Laptop Guidelines

Certified Pre-Owned Grade A Laptops

These devices look new like at the first glance, upon close examination you may find some minor signs of wear and tear.

Features of Grade A Laptops:

  • Excellent Condition
  • Minor Signs of Use
  • Fully Functional
  • Professionally Tested
  • No Scratches

Pre-Owned Grade B Laptops

These devices show obvious wear, dents and or scratches on corners and back. Screen will have light scratches. These devices look used. The scratches on the screen (if any) are light and will not be noticeable during normal use. There are no cracks/ chips or damage to these devices

Features of Grade B Laptops:

  • Fair Condition
  • Fully Functional
  • Professionally Tested
  • Signs of Usage

Pre-Owned Grade ASIS Laptops - Working Devices

These devices will have scratches, dents, damages and may have technical issues but will be working devices.
These devices are sold in ASIS condition with no cosmetic revisions.
