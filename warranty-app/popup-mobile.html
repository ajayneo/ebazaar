How to find IMEI Number ?

  • How to find IMEI Number on Android ?

    Open the Android Settings menu >
    Tap "About Phone"> Tap "Status"
    OR
    Open the dialer on your phone and type *#06# using the keypad

  • How to find IMEI Number on iPhone ?

    Open the Apple Settings on the phone >
    Tap “General” > Tap “About”
    OR
    Open the dialer on your phone and type *#06# using the keypad
