How to find Serial Number?

  • Guide to Find serial number (Laptops other than Apple)

    Option 1
    Find Serial Number printed on the rear surface of Laptop.

    Option 2
    Press Start -> Type "Run" -> Type "CMD" ->Type "WMIC BIOS GET SERIALNUMBER" ->Press Enter

  • Guide to Find Serial Number and Year Generation (Apple)

    Option 1
    Serial number is printed on the rear surface of Macbook

    Option 2
    The window that appears lists your computer's model name--for example, MacBook Air (13-inch, Early 2015)--and serial number.
